
# BLite: Branding & Identity Guidelines

## 1. The Core Identity

**BLite** is a modern, binary-first document store designed for embedded systems and AI-ready applications. The name plays on the synergy between **B** (BSON/Binary) and **Lite** (lightweight/embedded), while phonetically echoing the word **"Bright"** to suggest intelligence and clarity.

-   **Tagline:** _The Binary-Light Document Store for AI-Ready Apps._
    
-   **Mission:** To provide high-performance, local data storage that eliminates cloud dependency and latency.
    

## 2. Visual Identity & Logo Concept

The visual theme is defined by the **"Rising Sun"** concept, representing the dawn of a new era of local data.

-   **The Icon:** A stylized, minimalist rising sun or a hexagonal BSON data block integrated with a fast-moving spark.
    
-   **The Color Palette:** * **Primary:** "Blite Red" (Livid Amaranth / Deep Red).
    
    -   **Secondary:** Tech Blue, Cyan, and Slate Grey.
        
-   **Vibe:** Sophisticated, "Aurora" or "Red Dawn" aesthetic.
